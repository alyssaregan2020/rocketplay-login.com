To complete the KYC verification, you will typically be asked to submit copies of a few standard documents through a secure portal in your account profile. These documents usually include:

  • Proof of Address: A copy of a recent document (usually dated within the last three months) that shows your full name and address, such as a utility bill, bank statement, or official government letter.
